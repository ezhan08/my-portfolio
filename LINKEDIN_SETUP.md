# LinkedIn Data Integration Guide

## Profile Picture Setup

1. Save your profile picture as `profile-picture.jpg` in the `public/images/` directory
2. The image should be at least 400x400px for best quality
3. Recommended format: JPG or PNG

## LinkedIn Data Fetching

Once you're logged into LinkedIn, I can help fetch:
- Updated work experience
- Recent posts and images
- Skills and endorsements
- Recommendations
- Projects and publications

## Adding Images from LinkedIn Posts

1. Navigate to your LinkedIn profile
2. Open your posts
3. Right-click on images and save them to `public/images/posts/`
4. Update the portfolio data with image paths
